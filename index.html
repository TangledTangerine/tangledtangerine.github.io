About Me

Hey! I’m Tangled Tangerine an indie game developer who loves creating stylized, creative, and fun digital worlds. I experiment a lot, build weird ideas, and try to make games that feel fresh and full of personality. I work on everything from prototypes to full projects, always learning, polishing, and pushing my style forward.

My Journey

I started making games because I loved seeing ideas come to life on screen. Over time, I went from tiny experiments to full-on projects, learning something new with every build. I’m always exploring new styles, mechanics, and ways to make my games stand out.

Philosophy

I believe games should feel alive-stylized visuals, smooth movement, satisfying feedback, and a strong sense of personality. Every project I work on is built around that idea: make it fun, make it clean, make it memorable.

Tools & Tech

I mainly work with Python and Pygame, creating crisp 2D games with tight controls and polished art. I keep experimenting with shaders, animation techniques, and systems to push my projects further.
